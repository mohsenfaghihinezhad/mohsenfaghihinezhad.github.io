Overview

This teaching portfolio presents my development as an educator in environmental engineering. It includes my teaching philosophy, teaching experience, course design materials, workshop reflections, and evidence toward the Certificate in College Teaching.

My teaching interests include environmental engineering, environmental chemistry, water and wastewater treatment, emerging contaminants, advanced treatment systems, and bioremediation. I am especially interested in helping students connect technical concepts to real environmental problems.
